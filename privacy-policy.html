Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

Privacy & Security

Pee Tracker is designed with your privacy as our top priority. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled within the app.

Data Storage

All your health data is stored securely on your device and in your private iCloud account. We never access or sell your data.

  • All data is stored locally on your device first
  • SwiftData provides secure, encrypted local storage
  • Your data never leaves your personal ecosystem
  • No remote servers or third-party databases are used

CloudKit Sync

Data syncs between your devices using Apple's CloudKit framework with end-to-end encryption.

  • Synchronization happens through your private iCloud account
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Only your devices with the same Apple ID can access synced data
  • We have no access to your CloudKit data
  • You can disable sync at any time in iOS Settings

Data Sharing

You control all data exports. We never automatically share your information with third parties.

  • Export features are entirely manual and user-initiated
  • You choose what data to export and where to share it
  • Exports are available in CSV and PDF formats
  • No automatic uploads or third-party integrations
  • Your health data is yours alone

No Third-Party Access

Pee Tracker does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

  • No analytics frameworks (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No third-party SDKs with data collection
  • No crash reporting that sends data externally
  • No behavioral tracking

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time.

  • Delete individual sessions from the History view
  • Delete all data through Settings â†’ Delete All Data
  • Deletion is immediate and permanent
  • Deleted data is also removed from CloudKit sync

Health Information Disclaimer

While this app helps you track urinary health information, it is designed for personal use only and does not constitute medical advice.

  • Always consult healthcare professionals for medical concerns
  • The app provides informational insights, not diagnoses
  • Health insights are educational and not a substitute for medical care
  • Critical symptoms should prompt immediate medical attention

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact

Summary: Your health data is private, secure, and entirely under your control. We build privacy into every aspect of Pee Tracker, using Apple's secure frameworks and never accessing, selling, or sharing your personal information.
