About Pee Tracker

Pee Tracker is a comprehensive iOS and watchOS application designed to help men monitor their urination patterns and symptoms through a structured pee diary. Whether you're tracking bladder health, monitoring medical conditions, or simply want to understand your hydration patterns better, Pee Tracker provides the tools you need.

Built with privacy in mind, all your data is securely stored in your personal iCloud account and syncs seamlessly between your iPhone and Apple Watch, ensuring your health information stays private and accessible only to you.

How It Works

  • Start a session on your iPhone or Apple Watch when you need to use the bathroom
  • The timer automatically tracks the duration of your session
  • When finished, record how you felt and any symptoms you experienced
  • Add optional notes about medications, fluids consumed, or other relevant details
  • Review your history to identify patterns and share insights with your healthcare provider
  • All data automatically syncs between your devices via iCloud

Privacy & Data

Your privacy is our top priority. Pee Tracker stores all data locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account using CloudKit's private database. This means:

  • We never have access to your health data
  • Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Only you can access your information through your Apple ID
  • No third-party analytics or tracking
  • No advertisements or data sharing

System Requirements

  • Apple Watch running watchOS 10.0 or later (optional)
  • iCloud account with iCloud Drive enabled
  • Internet connection for sync (WiFi or Cellular)
